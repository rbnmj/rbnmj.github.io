About Me

Hi, I'm Ruben, a dedicated ecologist with a passion for sustainable development and ecosystem conservation. My work involves environmental assessments, stakeholder engagement, and developing adaptive strategies to ensure the preservation of our natural environment. I have a strong background in spatial analysis, data science, and project management. Although remote sensing is my strong suit, I also enjoy occasional fieldwork for biotope or faunistic mapping.

When I'm not working on hands-on projects, you can find me dabbling in dynamical models, AI, or cryptography. Deep diving into complex and innovative topics helps me continuously sharpen my strategic problem-solving skill in a fun way.
Always open for projects and collaboration.

EcoRSS

Simple RSS feed of many scientific journals on all aspects of ecology and nature conservation. However, there is absolutely a bias towards more theoretical publications. Some more general journals with high impact are included as well. A list of all currently included journals and their respective rss link can be found in the repository. The feed runs on osmosfeed, an RSS reader running on GitHub Pages with periodical updates via GitHub Actions.
